What is KUSARI?

Kusari (meaning CHAIN in Japanese) is the canary network to the SwapDEX Chain. It is a decentralized platform bringing the very best tried and tested financial products to market through the Kusari blockchain. The idea behind the canary network is to provide an operational blockchain with a defined token supply that is intended to be used to test features for a related mainnet. The most interesting part about Kusari is that it mimics an almost network replica of SwapDEX including having real economic value. Kusari operates for SwapDEX as a live testing environment / proving ground for development teams deploying projects, upgrading runtimes and upgrades on-chain Governance decisions.

One of Kusari's great features is the Governance whereby in true decentralized fashion the blockchain is governed by the community, treasury and council members that owns at least 1 KSI. The Governance spans across several different features. Examples could include increasing the transactions per second (TPS), decommissioning of bad actors (validators), changes to the amount of needed validator resources and the commissioning of bounties. Changes are all deployed seamlessly onto Kusari first before being implemented on the SwapDEX chain to keep the main chain free of any technical issues after the need for upgrades or updates. Other projects and development teams who want to come onto SwapDEX ecosystem have the option of deploying on the Kusari network first to be rubber stamped into the SwapDEX ecosystem as a trusted DApp from the community.

Why MetaMask And Substrate?

MetaMask and Substrate are Google Chrome, Brave, Opera and Firefox extensions for the browser and a standalone Android and iPhone app which makes it easy for web applications to communicate with the Ethereum blockchain.

In other words, MetaMask and Substrate are wallets for your browser to interact with our chain and all our, or your, future applications.
